Privacy Policy

Butce Uzmani Mobile Application
Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Butce Uzmani mobile application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your personal data. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

Summary: Your financial data belongs solely to you. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for marketing. You can delete your account anytime directly from within the app. The free version may display ads in future versions; the Premium version shows no ads at all.

1. App Owner

Butce Uzmani is a personal finance application independently developed and operated by Hakan Doner.

Contact Email: butceuzmani.feedback@gmail.com

Developer: Hakan Doner

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address (when you sign in with Google or Apple)
  • Display name
  • Account creation date
  • Device platform (Android / iOS)

2.2 Financial Data

For the core functionality of the app, you provide the following data yourself:

  • Income and expense records (amount, category, date, description)
  • Asset records (cash, foreign currency, gold, silver, crypto, stocks)
  • Monthly budget limits and category-based goals
  • Savings goals
  • Fixed monthly expenses
  • Custom categories and subcategories you create

2.3 App Usage Data

  • Daily login streak
  • Notification preferences
  • Push notification token (used only to deliver notifications)

2.4 Advertising ID (To Be Added Later)

In future versions, ads will be shown to free users. When this feature is active, Google AdMob may collect your device's Advertising ID and basic device information (device model, operating system version, approximate country) to show you more relevant ads. Premium users will see no ads and no advertising ID will be collected from them.

The app does not use analytics, behavioral tracking, or cookies. We do not track your user behavior.

3. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used only for the following purposes:

  • Providing the core features of the app (expense tracking, reports, budget management)
  • Syncing your data across multiple devices
  • Sending notifications (only if you have granted permission)
  • Troubleshooting technical issues
  • Displaying ads in the free version (in future versions, only for free users)

We never sell your financial data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for marketing purposes. Our advertising service only uses device-level advertising identifiers and does not have access to your financial data.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Is It Stored?

Your financial data is stored encrypted on Google Firebase (Firestore) infrastructure. Servers are located in Google's European data centers (Belgium).

4.2 Who Can Access It?

  • Only you - Firestore security rules allow access only to your own account's data
  • No staff member accesses your data unless legally required

4.3 Data Stored Only on Your Device

Some data is stored only on your device for performance reasons and is not sent to any server:

  • Recurring payment reminders
  • Exchange rate cache (1 hour)
  • Crypto price cache (5 minutes)
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID / fingerprint - never leaves your device)

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

5.1 Firebase (Google LLC)

Used for authentication and database services. Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5.2 Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In

Used for account creation and sign-in. Through these services, only your email address and display name are accessed.

5.3 EmailJS

When you send feedback from within the app, your message is delivered via EmailJS. Your financial data does not pass through this service; only your submitted message text and contact details are transmitted.

5.4 TCMB (Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey)

The public TCMB API is used to fetch current exchange rates. No personal data is sent to this service.

5.5 CoinGecko

The public CoinGecko API is used to fetch current cryptocurrency prices. No personal data is sent to this service.

5.6 Google AdMob (To Be Added Later)

In future versions, Google AdMob will be used to show ads to free users. AdMob may collect your device's Advertising ID, IP address, and basic device information to show you relevant ads. Banner ads and optional rewarded video ads will be displayed.

You can completely remove ads by upgrading to Premium. You can also reset your Advertising ID or disable personalized ads through your device settings.

AdMob Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and Turkish KVKK laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Learn what data is stored about you
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to data portability: Receive a copy of your data
  • Right to object: Object to data processing

7. Account and Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time directly from within the app:

  • Go to the Profile screen
  • Tap "Delete Account"
  • Confirm

When your account is deleted, all your financial data (incomes, expenses, assets, categories) is immediately removed from Firestore. It may remain briefly in Firebase's automatic backup system, but per Google's policy, these backups are permanently deleted after a certain period.

If you encounter any issues, you can also send your deletion request to the email address below.

8. Children's Privacy

Butce Uzmani is designed for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we become aware that a user under 13 has an account, we will delete the account and related data.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When significant changes occur, we will notify you from within the app. The most recent version will always be available on this page.

10. Contact

For any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy, please contact us using the email address below. You can use this address for deletion requests, data access requests, and any other matters.

Email: butceuzmani.feedback@gmail.com

Developer: Hakan Doner

Response time: We typically respond within 3 business days.
