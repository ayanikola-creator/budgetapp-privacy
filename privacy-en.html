Privacy Policy

Bütçe Uzmanı – Personal Finance App

Last updated: April 2026
We respect your data. This policy explains what data the Bütçe Uzmanı app collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Data Collected

The app collects and processes the following data:

  • Account information (email address, display name) — when signing in with Google, Apple, or email
  • Expense and income records (category, amount, date)
  • Budget goals and category limits
  • App preferences (currency, notification settings)

2. Purpose of Data Usage

Collected data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing financial tracking and reporting features
  • Cross-device data synchronization
  • Personalized notifications and suggestions

Your data is never used for advertising, profiling, or analytics purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure with industry-standard encryption. Firebase holds SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications.

In-app PIN is hashed using the SHA-256 algorithm and stored on your device. Biometric data is kept only on your device. No security data is ever sent to the server.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Your data is never shared, sold, or rented to any third party unless required by law.

Third-party services used:

  • Google Firebase — data storage and authentication
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In — optional sign-in methods

5. Right to Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all data via Profile > Security and Data > Delete Account. This action cannot be undone.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for users under 13 years of age, and no data is knowingly collected from this age group.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. In case of significant changes, an in-app notification will be sent. The current policy is always available on this page.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, you can reach us through the in-app Feedback screen or at the following email address:
